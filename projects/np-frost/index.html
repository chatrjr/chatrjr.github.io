  • Design

    As a designer, my focus rests on three things: the craft, the message, and you (the visitor). I play with ideas and stretch their limits to see what works best.

  • Development

    I give the work a flexible platform to grow and thrive upon. I make sure you don’t experience crawling load times, performance bottlenecks, or interfaces that make you swear.

  • Heart

    My work is made to be used, and sometimes for the joy of making. Every line of text is written to connect. Every choice hinges on making wonderful experiences for awesome people. You’re not users, pageviews, or a market share.

    3 Step Cycle

  • Think

    Every design starts with an idea. When held and tossed, stretched and tested, an idea can mature into a concept. It’s the glue that holds a design together, linking form and message.

  • Build

    Next, I begin with sketches, and then I move quickly to the real canvas. The project decides the platform. A simple site will have very different needs from a data-driven behemoth.

  • Review

    Finally, I gather and organize feedback to decide how to move forward, or whether to pull back. A better project might mean starting from scratch at times.
