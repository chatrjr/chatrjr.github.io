Yo, I’m Chatman.

I’ve been flitting around the tech world like a mad hummingbird for a while. Most of my work is web related, but I feed a broader interest in programming and systems. A curiosity about what I use every day boiled over into action with an internship as the starting pistol. And hell, I just get a lot of joy out of experimental play.

You’ll most often find me reading for knowledge or pleasure. Being somewhat severely introverted, I’ve also used it to shield my personal space. Video games account for a good chunk of my time as well. I’ve played probably too many Japanese and western RPGs, enjoy a good puzzle, and I’ve been a 2D fighter since the SNES.

My humble home on the internet is a scratchpad of my rough and finished ideas and projects. Finished in the sense that I’ve stopped modifying them. I’m unapologetic about how often I go off on tangents to cement my understanding or pick at specific problems. You can expect a lot of works-in-progress, self-correction, and rather frequent rejection of my outmoded ideas. Sometimes all at once.

Other tidbits.

I do most of my tinkering on the web at CodePen and JSFiddle. My source code is hosted at Github and maintained privately on BitBucket.

You can get the source of this site and details about its creation and growth.

Additionally, the typography is set in: Oswald and Roboto for headlines and body copy, Source Code Pro for code.
